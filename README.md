# No Sweat Community Policy

This Policy outlines the standards, expectations, and enforcement practices for all members of the **No Sweat** community.  
By accessing or participating in No Sweat platforms, services, or events, you agree to comply with this Policy.

---

## 1. Purpose

No Sweat exists to provide a competitive yet respectful environment for Fortnite players.  
This Policy is designed to promote fairness, inclusivity, accountability, and a positive community experience.

---

## 2. Communication Standards

All forms of communication within No Sweat platforms must remain respectful and appropriate.

- Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or threats are prohibited
- Excessive spam, flooding, or disruptive behavior is not permitted
- Explicit, NSFW, or offensive content is strictly forbidden
- Friendly banter is allowed; toxic behavior is not

---

## 3. Competitive Conduct

Members are expected to compete with integrity and sportsmanship.

- Cheating, exploiting, or use of unauthorized software is prohibited
- Account sharing or boosting is not allowed
- Intentional throwing, griefing, or sabotage is prohibited
- Clan strategies and internal information must not be shared externally

---

## 4. Recruitment & Rank Progression

No Sweat uses a structured progression system to maintain quality and fairness.

- Advancement through ranks is earned through activity, conduct, and performance
- Promotions are determined by staff and are not subject to public debate
- Inactivity or misconduct may result in demotion or removal
- Trial and trainee periods must be completed as outlined by staff

---

## 5. Access Control

Certain areas, communications, or resources may be restricted by rank or role.

- Restricted access is granted based on trust and responsibility
- Sharing or leaking restricted content is prohibited
- Abuse of privileges may result in immediate disciplinary action

---

## 6. Staff Authority

Staff members are responsible for enforcing this Policy.

- Staff decisions are final
- Circumventing moderation actions is prohibited
- Impersonation of staff members is not allowed
- Abuse toward staff will not be tolerated

---

## 7. Reporting & Compliance

Members are encouraged to report violations responsibly.

- Reports must be truthful and made in good faith
- False or malicious reporting is a violation of this Policy
- Failure to comply with moderation requests may result in enforcement action

---

## 8. Enforcement Actions

Violations of this Policy may result in one or more of the following actions:

- Warning
- Temporary restriction or mute
- Rank removal or demotion
- Removal from the community
- Permanent ban for severe or repeated violations

Enforcement actions are applied at the discretion of staff.

---

## 9. External Platform Compliance

All members must comply with applicable platform rules, including but not limited to:
- Discord Terms of Service
- Fortnite / Epic Games rules and policies

---

## 10. Policy Updates

This Policy may be updated at any time to reflect changes in community standards or operations.  
Continued participation in No Sweat following updates constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

---
